## For the agent

1. Read `BROCHURE-SITE-RULES.md` first.
2. Follow the kickoff interview in §1 before building anything.
3. Let the client brief decide the page set — do not assume fixed pages.
4. Keep all business facts in one data file; never hardcode them in components.
